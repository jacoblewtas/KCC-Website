About Us

We are a cafe and second hand clothing shop in Bootle, Liverpool who are passionate about giving people a safe space and a place to unwind. We offer a warm environment for everybody, from those who have lost their children to social services to people who suffer from mental health.

We run a wide range of groups and services used by all ages that benefit emotional and social wellbeing.

Once you are done browsing in our shop, why don't you pop next door to our cafe for a cuppa and a chat?

Whether you want to talk about the weather or life's problems, we are available to listen.
Get involved. Buy yourself a coffee, drop into one of our many sessions or tell us your suggestions.

You are vital to the success of the club.

Everybody is welcome, check out our wide range of groups and activities!(most of which are free!)

We always welcome donations at KCC. Click on the link above to donate online. Alternatively, pop into the preloved shop or the cafe and make a purchase, or simply donate in person.

Thank you to each and everyone one of you for your constant love and support.
